statement:

Hey there, if you're reading this it's because you followed my hard link. A few years back I realised that actively using and having social media was driving me insane. So I deactivated them. I went from constantly feeling watched and observed to being—often happily—out of the loop. When I'm thinking of someone now I often just reach out to them and ask. It feels nice and I intend to keep doing it.

I don't really plan on posting regularly here. Or even really calling what I'm doing here "posting" per se. The idea of doing anything explicitly to post still feels a bit fraught to me, but I do want to live in a world where people share without expectations or feedback. So maybe that is still posting and I'm just being angsty. Who knows. We've long since left my original point behind.

Outside my ramblings I'd like to thank you for showing the care and follow-through to actually visit something I've worked on. I can never thank you enough.
